Our Work Process

We follow a structured approach to ensure each project is completed efficiently and exceeds your expectations.

1

Discovery Call

We begin with an initial consultation to understand your project goals, requirements, and vision.

2

Proposal & Agreement

After our consultation, we'll provide a detailed proposal outlining scope, timeline, and cost. Upon approval, we'll formalize our agreement.

3

Project Planning

We'll create a comprehensive plan including storyboards, shot lists, and production schedules to ensure smooth execution.

4

Initial Payment

A deposit payment secures your project slot in our schedule and allows us to begin the creative process.

5

Content Collection

We'll gather all necessary materials, footage, assets, and information needed for your project.

6

Production Phase

Our team works diligently to create your project within the established timeline, usually completed within 48-72 hours.

7

Review & Revision

We'll share the first draft for your review. You'll have two rounds of revisions to ensure the final product meets your expectations.

8

Final Delivery

After all revisions are complete, we'll deliver the final files in your preferred format and resolution.

9

Follow-up & Support

We'll check in to ensure you're satisfied with the deliverables and provide any necessary support for implementation.
